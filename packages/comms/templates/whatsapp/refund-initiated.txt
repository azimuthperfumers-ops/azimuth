Language: English
Category: Utility

Body:
Hi {{customer_name}}, your refund of *{{amount}}* for order *{{order_number}}* has been initiated to {{refund_destination}}. Thank you for shopping with Azimuth Perfumers.

Variables:
{{customer_name}}       = customer name
{{order_number}}        = order number
{{amount}}              = amount (e.g. ₹1,234)
{{refund_destination}}  = where the money went, sent by code as one of:
                          "your Azimuth Wallet (store credit, available right away)"
                          "your original payment method — bank/card (5–7 business days)"

NOTE: the existing approved template on MSG91 must be EDITED to add
{{refund_destination}} (re-approval required) — otherwise the send fails with
a variable-count mismatch.
